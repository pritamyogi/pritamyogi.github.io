Hello, I am

Pritam Yogi.

I am a dedicated technical professional with over a decade of experience in managing and operating advanced electronics and characterization laboratories at premier academic and research institutes. I work closely with students, researchers, and faculty to support teaching, experimentation, and applied research through hands-on engagement.

Current Focus

In my current work, I focus on sensors and MEMS-based systems, custom PCB design and prototyping, additive manufacturing (3D printing), and digital and analog circuit development.

I also develop drone and robotic solutions for research labs and industry, with system integration involving MEMS sensors for temperature, pressure, gas, and piezo applications using modern embedded platforms.

Research Background

Earlier in my career, I contributed extensively to device-level research on ultra-scaled semiconductor devices, on-wafer characterization, and device reliability, including radiation effects and hot-carrier degradation.

DC & RF Measurements Cryo Characterization Nano Fabrication FESEM & EDX TCAD Modeling BSIM-BULK

Philosophy

I enjoy building reliable systems, setting up robust experiments, and mentoring students through practical learning, guided by strong fundamentals and careful measurement.
